Find what's wasting your space

Five smart detection engines work together to surface everything you don't need — so you can clean up without thinking.

🔍

Duplicate Detection

Uses perceptual hashing to find exact and near-exact copies — even if they were saved at different sizes or edited slightly. Groups them and recommends the best one to keep.

🔄

Similar Photos

Groups photos that look alike — burst shots, multiple takes, same scene. Shows a similarity score so you can decide which to keep and which to trash.

🌫️

Blurry Photos

Automatically detects blurry, out-of-focus, or low-quality shots. See a blur score for each photo and filter by severity — no more scrolling through unusable pictures.

📱

Screenshots

Finds all screenshots and groups them by time period — Today, This Week, This Month, Older. Quickly clear the ones you don't need anymore.

🎬

Videos

Lists all videos sorted by file size, largest first. See duration, resolution, and file size before you decide to delete. Reclaim serious space in seconds.

✨

Smart Recommendations

Every group auto-recommends the best photo to keep — highest resolution, largest file, oldest timestamp. One tap to select all the rest for deletion.
