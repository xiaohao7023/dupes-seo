## Long Description

**Your photo library is a mess. Dupes cleans it up — privately.**

We all have them: dozens of nearly identical photos from the same burst, accidental screenshots, blurry rejects, and videos we forgot about. Over time, they eat up precious storage and make it impossible to find the photos that matter.

**Dupes** scans your entire photo library and surfaces:

- 🔍 **Exact duplicates** — same photo saved twice (or ten times)
- 🎨 **Similar photos** — near-identical shots so you can pick the best one
- 🌫️ **Blurry photos** — out-of-focus images taking up space
- 📱 **Screenshots** — those recipe links and shipping confirmations
- 🎬 **Duplicate videos** — same clip saved from different sources

**Everything stays on your device.** Dupes processes your photos entirely on-device using Apple's native photo APIs. Nothing is uploaded, nothing is tracked, nothing is collected. Your memories never leave your iPhone.

**Simple, honest pricing.** Scan up to 100MB of duplicates for free. When you're ready to clean it all up, grab a one-time lifetime purchase — no subscriptions, no recurring fees.

Whether you're running out of iCloud storage, preparing for a new iPhone, or just want a tidy photo library, Dupes makes it effortless.

## Maker Comment

As a solo developer, I wanted to build the photo cleaner I'd actually trust with my own library. Privacy-first isn't a marketing slogan here — it's an architectural decision. All scanning and comparison happens locally using iOS machine learning frameworks. There's no analytics SDK, no crash reporting that sends data off-device, nothing.

If you try it out, I'd genuinely love to hear what you think — especially around the UX of reviewing and deciding which duplicates to keep. Thanks for checking it out! 🙏
